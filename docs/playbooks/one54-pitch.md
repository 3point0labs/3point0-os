## Positioning — what to say in a pitch

- **Do say:** "insider-led coverage," "operator audience," "reaches decision-makers across the African business ecosystem"
- **Don't say:** "Africa is emerging" (cliché), "underserved market" (patronizing), generic reach numbers without context
- **Always:** tie the sponsor's product to a specific episode or category angle — see `one54-target-sponsors.md`

## Agent instructions

When drafting outreach, the agent should:
1. Reference one specific, recent thing about the sponsor company (from web search)
2. Tie it to One54's audience with a concrete angle, not a generic "great fit"
3. Suggest one clear next step (15-min call, deck review, etc.)
4. Keep it 3 paragraphs max
