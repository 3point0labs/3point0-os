## Tier definitions

- **Tier S:** Dream sponsors. Drop everything to pitch. Budget >$50k/deal likely. Multi-episode or season-long potential.
- **Tier A:** Strong fits. Pitch with intent. $10-50k deal range.
- **Tier B:** Decent fits. Volume pitch — include in quarterly waves, don't overinvest per contact.

---

## Per-contact angles by title

- **CEO / Founder:** Story + mission. Don't lead with CPM. Talk about positioning.
- **CMO / Head of Marketing:** Audience match + distribution. Show you understand their funnel.
- **Head of Partnerships / BD:** Integration possibilities beyond a simple ad read.
- **Comms / PR lead:** Earned-media narrative angle — guest appearance exchange, content collabs.
