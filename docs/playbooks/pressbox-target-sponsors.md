## Active target categories

### 1. Sports betting / fantasy (Tier S)

**Why they fit:** Biggest ad spend in sports media. Pressbox audience = engaged bettors and fantasy players.

### 3. Athletic apparel / performance brands (Tier A)

**Why they fit:** Story-driven audience. Brand identity matters more than pure reach.

### 4. Sports law / agent services / athlete financial advisors (Tier A)

**Why they fit:** NFLPA pipeline = direct audience of athletes, agents, and families planning money moves.

**Example companies:** [FILL IN — Richey May Sports Accounting, Kessler & Solomiany, AWM Capital, OL Reign-style athlete-focused RIAs?]

### 5. Sports media / team partnerships (Tier A)

**Why they fit:** Cross-promotion, content trades, not just cash ads.

**Example companies:** [FILL IN — Meadowlark Media, Overtime, The Players' Tribune, team-owned media properties, local sports media networks?]

**Angle:** Co-produced content, guest swaps, distribution trades. Easier to close than cash deals.

### 7. Sports-focused financial / crypto (Tier B — cautious)

**Example companies:** [FILL IN — legit-only list. Vetted RIAs, Robinhood's athlete products, Cash App's sports partnerships?]

## Categories we don't pitch

- **Africa-focused business products:** Wrong fit. Send to One54.
- **Unlicensed gambling operators:** Legal + brand risk.
- **Daily-fantasy platforms with lottery-style structures targeting casual bettors:** reputational risk.
- **Supplement brands without NSF/Informed Sport certification:** athletes can't use them safely.

## Tier definitions

- **Tier S:** Dream sponsors. Drop everything to pitch. $50k+ deals likely. Multi-episode or season potential.
- **Tier A:** Strong fits. Intentional pitch, personalized.
- **Tier B:** Decent fits. Volume-pitch in quarterly waves.

---

## Per-contact angles by title

- **CEO / Founder:** Mission + story. Sports execs respond to authenticity — lead with why Pressbox is different, not reach numbers.
- **CMO / VP Marketing:** Audience quality + storytelling integration. Show the difference vs generic sports-pod placements.
- **Head of Athlete Partnerships / NIL Lead:** Pitch as an athlete-access channel, not a media buy.
- **Agent / Player Rep:** Episode guest exchange. Zero-dollar conversion that unlocks relationships downstream.
- **Sports law firm partner:** Authority play — branded segment on contract/NIL questions.
